How Feedify Works

Three simple steps from surplus food to full plates

1
🍲

Donor Logs Surplus

Restaurants, hotels, and caterers register their surplus food with just a few taps — food type, quantity, and pickup window.

2
🤖

AI Matches to Nearest NGO

Our PAPS algorithm scores each donation and intelligently matches it with the most suitable NGO based on proximity, capacity, and food type.

3
❤️

Food Reaches People

NGOs claim and coordinate pickup. Meals are distributed to those who need them most, tracked end-to-end on the platform.
